If you hold MOVR in AirGap, you can delegate them to earn rewards following this guide.

## key considerations before staking

| Action               | Value         |
| -------------------- | ------------- |
| Locked while staking | Yes           |
| Undelegating period  | 2 days        |
| Rewards after        | 4 hours       |
| Slashing Risk        | No            |
| Payout               | Automatic     |
| Minimun              | 5 MOVR        |
| Max                  | 100 MOVR      |
| Auto-compound        | No            |
| Commission fee       | 20% of profit |

## How to stake MOVR in AirGap Wallet

- To begin staking Moonriver, first, ensure you have set up a MOVR account. If you already have a Moonriver account, you may want to skip the next five steps.
- Go to the vault, click on the arrow button at the bottom right, and click the plus button.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver5.jpg"} />
<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver4.jpg"} />

- Select the moonriver protocol and click the create button to add the MOVR protocol.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver3.jpg"} />

- Your MOVR account should be ready in the Vault. Sync the account to the wallet.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver1.jpg"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver10.jpg"} />

- The next page asks you how you want to interact with the wallet. For the offline mode, you will scan the QR-code. For the same device approach, click the same device button to launch and sync the wallet.
- Allow the balance to load up and click the import button.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver8.jpg"} />

- Now you've successfully created a MOVR account, you can fund your wallet and begin delegating.
- Open your wallet and select your Moonriver account.
- Click the delegation button

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/moonriver/moonriver7.png"} />

- On the next page, you can change the collator you wish to delegate to, and specify the amount MOVR token you want to delegate.
- Click the delegate button to continue the process.
