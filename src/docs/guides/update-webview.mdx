We encourage our users to use old android smartphones for the vault app, and one common challenge they face during the setup is that the Webview on these old devices 
is not up to date, and this leads to certain errors (usually the app opening and just showing a white screen).

On this page, we have a quick guide on how to update the android webview.

:::note Play Store required
To update the android system webview using this method your device needs to support the play store app.
:::

- Open the playstore app
- Search for “android system webview”

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/webview/1-android-webview.png"} />

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/webview/2-android-webview.png"} />

- Click the update button

:::note Update button unavailable
if update button is unavailable skip this step and repeat the same process for “google chrome”
:::
